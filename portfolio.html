About Me







Hello everyone, my name is Mustabshira Murtaza and I am a frontend developer. You might be wondering how old I am, well, I am only 10 years old. I know it's unusual for someone so young to be interested in coding and website development, but I can't help myself. I am homeschooler and I have been learning coding since the age of 8. I started with simple HTML and CSS, and gradually moved on to more complex coding languages such as Javascript. I know some people might find it hard to believe that someone so young could be a frontend developer, but age is just a number. I believe that if you have a passion for something and you work hard at it, then you can achieve great things regardless of your age. I love coding because it allows me to be creative and to develop new skills. I enjoy the challenge of creating websites that are visually appealing and interactive. Aside from coding, I love spending time with my family, playing games, and reading books. I am hoping to continue developing my skills as a frontend developer and to create websites that will inspire others. Thank you for taking the time to read about me.
